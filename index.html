About Me

My first stab at coding was on a Radio-Shack Tandy TRS-80 I got for Christmas. Eventually, I dabbled in Visual Basic and SQL while securing an Art degree alongside an Information Systems Minor. Yes, I've heard the jokes. In the meantime, I've worked in a prison, on a cruise ship, but mostly tv stations and was once voted Production Employee of the Year. Currently, I'm a longtime Broadcast Technician transitioning into a Front-end Developer role.

I like pretending I can sew and take photos while my machine and cameras collect dust. I relax by watching Hallmark rom-coms. When I don't want to relax, I binge horror, violence, or something weird. I get a high on fixing/hacking tech gadgets, doing absolutely nothing in the company of friends, and eating meat and potato style meals away from home. I love learning new stuff...current obsession, personal finance tidbits to quench my minimalist thirst.
